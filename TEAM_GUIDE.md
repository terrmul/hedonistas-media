# Hedonistas Media Library — Team Guide

Your searchable home for all Hedonistas photos and videos.

## Getting in

- **Website:** https://media.hedonistasmezcal.com
- **Log in** with the email and password Terry set up for you.
- Forgot your password? Click **Forgot password** on the login screen — a reset link comes to your email.

## Finding what you need

- **Search** — type what you're looking for in the search box (e.g. `bottle beach`, `agave field`, `bar cart`). Every photo and video is auto-tagged by subject, setting, colors, and mood, so plain descriptions work.
- **File type** — filter to just images, just video, or a specific format.
- **Format** — filter by shape: Landscape, Portrait, Square, 16:9, 9:16, 4:3. Handy when you need something sized for a specific post or print.
- **Sort** — newest or oldest first, or filter to a date range.

## Using an asset

- Click any thumbnail to open it larger, with its tags and description.
- **Download** the original full-resolution file with the download button.
- Videos play in the browser — scrub to find the moment you want.

## Adding files

- Drag and drop images or video onto the upload box in the sidebar (or click to browse).
- Supported formats: JPG, PNG, WEBP, HEIC, TIFF, MP4, MOV, M4V.
- New files added to the shared Dropbox folder appear automatically — no action needed.

## Questions

Anything not working, or a file that should be here but isn't? Message Terry.
